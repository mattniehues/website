Gamble Garden Rebrand

A historic landmark of Palo Alto, Gamble Garden is an oasis of tranquility and beauty in heart of busy Silicon Valley. Playing off of the sights and sounds of the garden, we created a fresh look to attract its tech-savvy visitors and bees alike.

Designed at Personify.

Stationery Set

The bearded iris is a mainstay of the new brand, bred in honor of founder Elizabeth F. Gamble. The iris, along with other illustrated garden dwellers, are applied to stationery, website, and marketing materials. The garden was also a source of inspiration for color.

Designed at Personify.
