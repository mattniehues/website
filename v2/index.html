A historic landmark of Palo Alto, Gamble Garden is an oasis of tranquility and beauty in heart of busy Silicon Valley. Playing off of the sights and sounds of the garden, we created a fresh look to attract tech-savvy visitors and bees alike.

Camp EDMO

After 10+ years of serving kids all across the Bay Area, Camp Edventure More needed to reposition itself. We started by shortening the name down to EDMO — a nickname invented by veteran campers — and created a new logo and identity to speak to the maker/tech influenced summer programs.

Alkalign

Alkalign is a barre fitness studio based on the principles of strength, alignment and nutrition. Their manifesto encourages clients to find their own personal balance; “We practice what is proven but believe forward motion brings stability.” From there we set out to create a bold mark and look that represents this message.

MoxieMade

At Personify we were tasked to develop a name, voice, identity, and website for MoxieMade, a bespoke furniture maker offering the designer edge to the mass produced furniture industry. With their bright fabrics and one-of-a-kind patterns in mind, we designed an identity system that lets their pieces sing.

Zip Brew Co.

Conceptualized around the idea of crafting flavorful beer mixtures, Zip Brew Co.’s story is told through whimsical illustrations of two unexpected characters being zipped together.


The packaging consists of one continuous piece of recycled cardboard which utilizes the weight of the bottles the secure themselves providing greater reinforcement during transportation compared to conventional beer packaging.
